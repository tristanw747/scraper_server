Covid-19 Data

Death and harm for millions of people worldwide. Effect on US economy: Labor shortages, disruption to supply chain.Reopening after lockdown means huge demand and high inflation Best resolution: Vaccination + strain severity diminishes Turn for the worse: New strain more infectious and deadly, or vaccine not effective

Russia/Ukraine War

Severe energy shortage due to western sanctions against Russia for starting war with Ukraine. Effect on US economy: High gas prices Best resolution: War ends, sanctions end, new sources of energy Turn for the worse: war escalates, nuclear weapons used
